Why We Exist

Our Purpose

We exist to help organizations across industries design, plan, and operate business functions that are resilient, responsive, and economically optimal — spanning supply chain, sales, and manufacturing — while simultaneously developing professionals who can think across business, data, and technology boundaries.

The highest-leverage professionals of the next decade are translators — people who can move between business context, analytical modelling, and AI-enabled execution. McKinsey calls them translators. BCG calls them T-shaped professionals. Palantir calls them Forward Deployed Engineers.

Our Model

The Forward-Deployed Expert (FDE) Model

Palantir pioneered the Forward Deployed Engineer. McKinsey, Bain, and BCG have always embedded experts with clients. What is new is that AI fluency is now a prerequisite for this role.

The Optimodel FDE model trains practitioners as domain-fluent, analytics-capable, AI-literate professionals who can be deployed directly into client environments to solve real problems — not deliver slide decks from a distance.

Over time, FDE practitioners build portfolios of applied work that make them highly marketable — and their real-world experience feeds back into the community as case studies, refined templates, and richer training content.
