Our Model

The Forward-Deployed Expert (FDE) Model

The best consulting happens inside your team — not above it. Optimodel embeds domain-fluent, AI-literate experts who build, coach, and deliver alongside you. Faster adoption. Stronger capability transfer. Solutions that actually stick.

"The best consulting happens when experts work alongside your team — not above it."

1

Problem Definition Sprint

Structured diagnostic using MBB issue-tree methodology — define the problem precisely before any analysis begins.

2

Embedded Deployment

An Optimodel expert joins your team — on-site or remote — with full access to your data, systems, and stakeholders.

3

Build & Analyse

We build models and develop recommendations using proven templates — with your team alongside, not watching from the sidelines.

4

Capability Transfer

We leave behind reusable frameworks and trained teams — not black-box outputs that only we can interpret.
