What You Will Be Able to Do

Key Learning Outcomes

Every module is built around the Decision Enhancement Framework — each skill tied to a real business decision it supports.

Inventory Science

Calculate safety stock, EOQ, and reorder points for real multi-SKU inventory scenarios.

Demand Forecasting

Build and evaluate forecasting models — from Holt-Winters to LightGBM — and interpret accuracy metrics.

Optimization

Formulate and solve LP / MILP problems using Python, PuLP, and OR-Tools.

S&OP Design

Design S&OP processes and manage consensus planning across commercial and supply functions.

AI Deployment

Deploy AI tools (Amazon Forecast, Claude API, Blue Yonder patterns) in real supply chain workflows.

Consulting Communication

Apply MBB frameworks to structure and present supply chain recommendations to senior stakeholders.
