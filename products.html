OptiStock

Multi-Echelon Inventory Optimization

Upload your SKU and location data to get science-based safety stock recommendations across your entire distribution network. Multi-echelon algorithms account for inter-echelon uncertainty and MDC buffer effects — no spreadsheet engineering required.

  • Multi-SKU, multi-location, multi-period optimization
  • Multi-echelon vs. single-echelon algorithm comparison
  • ABC-XYZ segmentation with tailored stocking strategies
  • Executive dashboard: DOI, CFR, SKU health monitor
  • What-if scenario planning for lead time & demand changes
  • Inventory glidepath with period-by-period trend analysis
  • Excel template workflow — upload, optimize, export
Beta — Available Now Request Access

RouteSolve

Transportation Route Optimization

Optimize vehicle routes across your supply chain network. Consolidate shipments, reduce transportation costs, and improve fleet utilization using Google OR-Tools CVRP solver — with full scenario modelling and visual before-and-after comparison.

  • Multi-depot capacitated vehicle routing (CVRP)
  • Shipment consolidation & multi-stop route detection
  • Backhaul optimization for return-leg utilization
  • Scenario management: add/remove nodes, adjust volumes & rates
  • Interactive map visualization with color-coded routes
  • Executive comparison dashboard: cost, distance & fleet KPIs
  • Lane-level drill-down and cross-scenario comparison
Beta — Available Now Request Access

AccessMap

Population Accessibility Analysis

Assess facility coverage and estimate population reach using isochrone analysis. Combine real travel-network data with WorldPop population estimates to answer: "How many people can reach each facility within X minutes?"

  • Isochrone service-area mapping (time or distance bands)
  • Multiple travel modes: car, HGV, cycling, walking
  • Population estimation via WorldPop raster & API data
  • Scenario comparison for facility network configurations
  • Interactive Leaflet maps with color-coded coverage zones
  • Bulk facility import from CSV/Excel
  • GeoJSON & CSV export for GIS integration
Beta — Available Now Request Access

DemandLens

Demand Forecasting Workbench

Upload historical demand data, run multiple forecasting models side-by-side, compare accuracy metrics, and generate a production-ready forecast.

  • Models: SES, Holt-Winters, ARIMA, LightGBM, Prophet
  • Automatic accuracy benchmarking (MAPE, RMSE, Bias)
  • Interactive forecast visualisation
  • Seasonality and trend decomposition
  • Export forecasts to Excel or CSV
Beta — Available Now Request Access

NetSolve

Supply Network Design Tool

Configure your network nodes, lanes, and costs. Run optimization to find the best facility location and flow allocation for your supply chain.

  • Node & lane configuration (factories, DCs, customers)
  • Cost modelling: fixed, variable, transport
  • LP / MILP solver under the hood (OR-Tools)
  • Scenario comparison: current vs. optimized vs. alternative
  • Visual network map output
Beta — Available Now Request Access

PlanAssist

AI-Powered S&OP Assistant

Ask questions in natural language about your supply plan. Get structured recommendations backed by your data. Your AI co-pilot for S&OP and IBP.

  • Natural language interface for supply plan queries
  • Connects to your demand, inventory, and supply data
  • Structured recommendations with data backing
  • Scenario simulation: "What if demand spikes 20%?"
  • Built on Anthropic Claude API
